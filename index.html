About me

My name is Maddie Laycock and I am a senior at Virginia Tech. I am double majoring in Human Development and Communication. In the fall of 2018 I was able to take a class that expanded my knowledge on visual media. I learned basic visual design skills for layouts, photography, websites and videos. I was able to recognize how to combine various communication media into a comprehensive and coherent message, and want to express my techniques and learnings to the public through this website.

For my fall photos, I wanted to take the approach of capturing bright colors and different fall foods. I focused a lot on close up images but added concepts like leading lines, framing and contrast to capture pictures that interest my audience. I hope you enjoy my work and please feel free to contact me with any questions or comments!
